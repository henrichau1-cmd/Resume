Figure Caption

Our p-value based on the paired t-test is 2.2e-16 which is less than our alpha value of 0.05. We reject the null hypothesis that being there is no difference in the means resting heart rate in BPM between cold showers and hot showers. This means that the difference between the two means is not 0. The t-score is 21.003 and our degrees of freedom is 79. Although we aren’t sure the exact value of the difference, there is a 95% confidence interval that the true difference of the means is between 31.37814 BPM and 37.94829 BPM.
